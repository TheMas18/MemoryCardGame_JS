How to play ?

Welcome to our captivating Memory Card Game, where you'll embark on a thrilling journey to test and hone your memory skills! Here's how to play:

  • Click on any card to reveal its hidden image.
  • Remember both the location and image of each card you uncover.
  • Click on a second card to reveal its image as well.
  • If the two cards match, they will remain face-up, and you'll earn points! Matching pairs earn you +5 points.
  • Keep flipping cards and matching pairs until you've uncovered all the matches on the grid.
  • But beware - every wrong guess will cost you precious time and points! Incorrect guesses result in a deduction of -5 points.
  • Additionally, there's a mistake limit. Each wrong move deducts 1 from the allotted number of mistakes.

Now, let's dive into the exciting modes this game offers:

  • Easy Mode: Enjoy a 15-second time limit with 3 allotted mistakes. Perfect for beginners or those seeking a quick challenge.
  • Medium Mode: Test your skills with a 30-second time limit and 4 allotted mistakes. Strike the right balance between speed and accuracy.
  • Hard Mode: Take on the ultimate challenge with a 60-second time limit and 3 allotted mistakes. Only the most skilled players will conquer this mode.
